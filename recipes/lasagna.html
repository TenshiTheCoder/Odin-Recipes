Lasagna Recipe

Description

Need a fun recipe for your family? Then look no further than this easy and delicious lasagna recipe

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles (uncooked or cooked, depending on the type)
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 jar (about 24 ounces) marinara sauce or spaghetti sauce
  • 1 egg (optional, for mixing with ricotta)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

    2. Preparing the meat:

  2. Toss until the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce.
  3. Add the marinara sauce to the meat and stir to combine. Let it simmer for a few minutes.

    4. Prepping the Ricotta mixture:

  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg (if using), dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

    5. Layering the Lasagna:

  5. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  6. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  8. Sprinkle with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese.
  9. Add another layer of meat sauce over the cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce.
  11. For the final layer, place the last 3 noodles on top, cover with the remaining meat sauce, and sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

    12. Baking the Lasagna:

  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can spray the foil with cooking spray).
  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can spray the foil with cooking spray).
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

    15. Let the Lasagna rest:

  15. Once done, remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set.

    16. Cut into your preferred sizes and serve hot. Enjoy!
