Lasagna Recipe

Description

Need a fun recipe for your family? Then look no further than this easy and delicious lasagna recipe

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles (uncooked or cooked, depending on the type)
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 jar (about 24 ounces) marinara sauce or spaghetti sauce
  • 1 egg (optional, for mixing with ricotta)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Preparing the meat:

    1: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage until browned. Drain any excess fat. 2: Add the marinara sauce to the meat and stir to combine. Let it simmer for a few minutes.

  3. Prepping the Ricotta mixture:

    1: In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg (if using), dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  4. Layering the Lasagna:

    1: In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. 2: Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. 3: Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles. 4: Sprinkle with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese. 5: Add another layer of meat sauce over the cheese. 6: Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. 7: For the final layer, place the last 3 noodles on top, cover with the remaining meat sauce, and sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

  5. Baking the Lasagna:

    1: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can spray the foil with cooking spray). 2: Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. 3: Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

  6. Let the Lasagna rest:

    1: Once done, remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set.

    7. Cut into your preferred sizes and serve hot. Enjoy!
